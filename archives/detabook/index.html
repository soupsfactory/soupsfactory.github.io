Bondeep, Bonoshreehotor Golpo - book design

OVERVIEW

My father has been writing short tales for children and publishing them in local Assamese publications for as long as I can remember. “Bondeep, Bonoshreehotor Golpo” (Bondeep, Bonoshree’s Tales) is a collection of short stories created and collected to raise awareness about the preservation of our natural environment. I consider myself fortunate to have assisted him with the typesetting andÂ design of this book. The book will be available at local bookstores on December 6, 2022. We intend to make it available online as soon as possible.
