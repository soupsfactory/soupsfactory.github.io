My Friend Mr Leakey

Tags

Guide
Tools used

Author J. B. S. Haldane and his book My Friend Mr Leakey always fascinated me. It has been one of my favorite books. During my Master's we were asked to redesign any of our favorite books. It came as a perfect opportunity to design it in a way I imagined the world of Mr Leakey would look like. I hope you enjoy this design as much as I loved working on it!
